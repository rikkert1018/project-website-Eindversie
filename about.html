About us.

We offer great bike tours outside the center of Amsterdam.

As a student in Amsterdam, I understand that the city can be overwhelming for newcomers. That's why I created an app for new students in Amsterdam. The app highlights hotspots that are popular among students and includes suggested routes through each neighborhood. Additionally, I've included routes outside of the city center that I personally created to showcase that Amsterdam has much more to offer than what people may think.
